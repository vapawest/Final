ART + TECH CLUB

What is the Art + Tech Club?

The Art + Tech Club (ATC) is a club started by student artists Haley Zuch, Valerie West, and Valeria Casas-Lopez originally to give students in the Electronic Arts track of the Fine Art degree a place to skillshare outside of class. It evolved into creating a learning environment for anyone of any major interested in the intersection of Art and Technology. We focus on important concepts to the art and technology canon, artists that paved the way for young undergrads like us, sharing skills like software demos, exploring what resources are available to us as UC Students, engaging in group critiques, and making friends.
